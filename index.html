About Me

Creative Coder & Developer

Hello everyone!

Welcome to my corner of the digital world! I am a passionate developer with a special focus on creative coding. I thrive on experimenting with the latest technologies and crafting innovative projects that push the boundaries of what’s possible. Over the years, I've honed my skills in SQL development, recognizing the paramount importance of data. However, the real excitement for me lies in transforming this data into meaningful, visually captivating presentations. It's a thrilling challenge to bring data to life in creative ways.

Beyond my technical pursuits, I have a deep interest in psychology and human behavior. I am driven by a profound desire to make the world a better place. With this goal in mind, I aim to highlight some of the critical issues facing our planet and explore their impact on our collective wellbeing.

Through this platform, I will shed light on several global issues, such as ocean conservation and its intricate connection to climate change. We will delve into the vital role of rainforests, their influence on ocean currents, and the micro-climate changes they trigger, like cyclones and storms. My mission is to raise awareness and inspire a sense of responsibility in the next generation so that we can all work together to create a healthier, more sustainable planet.

In addition to these topics, I am also passionate about exploring art through the lens of coding, especially with JavaScript. I hope you enjoy the artistic patterns I create as much as I enjoy making them.

Thank you for joining me on this journey. Let’s learn, create, and make a positive impact together!
